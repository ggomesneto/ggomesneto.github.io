The Long

Geraldo was born in Brazil. Since 2018, he lives in the USA. His career in the oilfield started when he was 21 and on the years after he worked in places like Siberia, Abu Dhabi, and Central America. All that excitement was not enough for him and, in 2018, he decided to pursue a dream and became a full-time poker player in Las Vegas. He succeeded for three years, and after getting married, decided to go back to work in a 'normal' job that could give him more financial stability. After working with different types of technology, he found love in coding and decided to take a step out of his comfort zone and started learning more about software engineering.
